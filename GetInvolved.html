Corporate Sponsorship

Companies wishing to support the Sandton SPCA can do so in many ways:

  • Hold a CSI day at our premises
  • Donations - click here for our wish list

If your company would like to support the Sandton SPCA please contact us on 011 444 7730, fax 011 444 8056 or email info@sandtonspca.org.za
